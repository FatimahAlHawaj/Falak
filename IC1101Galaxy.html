IC 1101 Galaxy
The largest known galaxy, located almost a billion light-years away, IC 1101 is the single largest discovered galaxy in the observatory universe. Just how large is it? At its largest pint, this galaxy extends about two million light-years from its core, and it has a mass of about a hundred trillion stars. Some estimates suggest that IC 1101 is about 6 million light-years in diameter. To give you some idea of what that means, the Milky Way is only a hundred thousand in diameter.
